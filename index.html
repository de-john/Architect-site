Hello my name is Dewitt and I currently reside in New York City. Growing up I always had a fascination with machinery, buildings and other manmade structures I would see on a daily basis. I wanted to know who created these structures, why they created it and how they work. I would attempt to deconstruct and reconstruct various objects mentally as well as physically to get an understanding of how they function. I could never see myself as just another user of a product, I wanted to know every detail of how a product functioned. I wanted to be a creator, to be able to work with my hands and my mind. Which is the reason why I became an Architect. As you browse through my site you will see several of my designs, both photos of some completed work as well as drawings for structures that are in the process of being built. Hope you enjoy.
